Note of five rupees issued during King Tribhuwan's rule

Pandit Janak Raj Pandey (1945 to 1948)

Pandit Bharat Raj Pandey (Sept 18, 1948 to Jan 22, 1952)

Pandit Narendra Raj Pandey (Jan 22, 1952 to April 22, 1956)

    Facts:

  • Domination: Mohru 5
  • Khajanchee:
      • 1 - Pandit Janak Raj Pandey
      2 - Pandit Bharat Raj Pandey
      3 - Pandit Narendra Raj Pandey
  • Printer: Indian Security Press, Nashik, India
  • Issued date: Between 1945 to 1953
  • Front: Portrait of King Tribhuwan & Guheshwari temple
  • Back: Tiger in a jungle
  • Watermark: King Tribhuwan wearing plumed crown
  • Size: 73 x 126 mm
