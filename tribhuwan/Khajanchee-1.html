Biography:

Pandit Janak Raj Pandey was the first Khajanchee of Nepal. He was the Khajanchee(Governor) from 1945 to 1948 during the ruling period of King Tribhuwan. Nepal Rastra Bank wasn't established at that time. So, the notes were issued as a MORU from Sadar Muluki Khana. For the first time domination of Moru 5, Moru 10 & Moru 100 were issued in Nepal. We can find the signature of Pandit Janak Raj Pandit on the above currency.
