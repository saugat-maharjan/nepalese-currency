Biography:

Bhekh Bahadur Thapa was the fourth governor of Nepal. He was the governor from August 14, l966 to July 26, l967 during the ruling period of King Mahendra. Now notes are issued as a Rs. Signature of Bhekh Bahadur Thapa can be seen on the notes of Rs 5, Rs 10 and Rs 100.
