Biography:

Dr. Yuba Raj Khatiwada was the 15th governor of Nepal. He was the governor from March 22, 2010 to March 19, 2015. Signatue of Dr. Yuba Raj Khatiwada can be seen on the currency of Rs 5, Rs 10, Rs 20, Rs 50, Rs 100, Rs 500 & Rs 1000. From his tenure issued year are kept in banknotes.
