Biography:

Bijaya Nath Bhattarai was the 13th governor of Nepal. He was the governor from (Jan 31, 2005 to June 29, 2007) (July 26, 2009 to March 22, 2010) during the ruling period of King Gyanendra. Signatue of Bijaya Nath Bhattarai can be seen on the currency of Rs 5, Rs 10, Rs 20, Rs 50, Rs 100, Rs 500 & Rs 1000.
