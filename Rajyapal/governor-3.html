Biography:

Pradyumna Lal Rajbhandari was the third governor of Nepal. He was the governor from June l8, l965 to August 13, l966 during the ruling period of King Mahendra. Notes were changed to Rs from Moru. Signature of Pradyumna Lal Rajbhandari can be seen on the notes of Rs 10 and Rs 100.
