Dr. Chiranjibi Nepal the was 16th governor of Nepal.He was the governor from March 19, 2015 - March 18, 2020. Signature of Dr. Chiranjibi Nepal can be seen on the currency of Rs 5, Rs 10, Rs 20, Rs 50, Rs 100, Rs 500 & Rs 1000.
