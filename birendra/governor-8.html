Biography:

Ganesh Bahadur Thapa was the 8th governor of Nepal.He was the governor from March 25, 1985 to May 25, 1990 during the ruling period of King Birendra.Signatue of Ganesh Bahadur Thapa can be seen on the currency of Rs 1, Rs 2, Rs 5, Rs 10, Rs 20, Rs 50, Rs 100, Rs 500 & Rs 1000.
