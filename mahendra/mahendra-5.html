Note of five rupees issued during King Mahendra's rule

Himalayan Shumsher JBR (April 26, 1956 to Feb 7, 1961)

Laxminath Gautam (Feb 8, 1961 to June 17, 1965)

Bhekh Bahadur Thapa (August 14, 1966 to July 26, 1967)

Yadav Prasad Pant (April 24, 1968 to April 28, 1973)

Yadav Prasad Pant (April 24, 1968 to April 28, 1973)
