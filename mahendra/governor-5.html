Biography:

Yadav Prasad Pant was the fifth governor of Nepal. He was the governor from April 24, l968 to April 28, l973 during the ruling period of King Mahendra. For the first time Rs 500 & Rs 1000 was issued. Signature of Yadav Prasad Pant can be seen on the currency notes of Rs 1 Rs 5, Rs 10, Rs 100, Rs 500 and Rs 1000.
