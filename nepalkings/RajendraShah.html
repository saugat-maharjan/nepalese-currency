Rajendra Bikram Shah Dev

Biography:

Rajendra Bikram Shah Dev was born on December 3, 1813 in Basantapur, Nepal. He was the son of Girvan Yuddha Bikram Shah Dev and Gorakshya Rajya Lakshmi Devi. He was the fifth King of Kingdom of Nepal from 20 November, 1816 to 12 May, 1847. He died at the age of 67 on July 10, 1881 in Bhaktapur, Nepal.
