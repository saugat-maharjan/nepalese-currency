Tilak Bahadur Rawal (Jan 31, 2000 to August 28, 2000)

Bijaya Nath Bhattarai (Jan 31, 2005 to June 29, 2007) (July 26, 2009 to March 22, 2010)

    Facts:

  • Domination: Rupees 5
  • Signature:
      • 12-Tilak Bahadur Rawal
      13-Bijaya Nath Bhattarai
  • Printer:
      • -De La Rue & Company, Ltd, UK
      -Perum Peruri, Indonesia
  • Issued date: Between 2002 to 2005
  • Front: Portrait of King Gyanendra & Temple of Goddess Taleju
  • Back: A pair of Yak, Mt. Everest
  • Watermark: Plumed crown
  • Size: 70 x 120 mm
