Note of five hundred rupees issued during King Gyanendra's rule

Tilak Bahadur Rawal (Jan 31, 2000 to August 28, 2000) (April 27, 2001 to Jan 17, 2005)

    Facts:

  • Domination: Rupees 500
  • Signature: 5-Tilak Bahadur Rawal
  • Printer: Giesecke & Devrient GMBH, Germany
  • Issued date: Between 2003 to 2006
  • Front: Portrait of King Gyanendra, Mt.Amadablam & Thyangboche Monastry
  • Back: A pair of Tigers
  • Watermark: Plumed crown
  • Size: 70 x 160 mm
