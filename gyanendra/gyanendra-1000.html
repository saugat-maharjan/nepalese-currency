Note of thousand rupees issued during King Gyanendra's rule

Tilak Bahadur Rawal (Jan 31, 2000 to August 28, 2000) (April 27, 2001 to Jan 17, 2005)

    Facts:

  • Domination: Rupees 1000
  • Signature: 5-Tilak Bahadur Rawal
  • Printer: Giesecke & Devrient GMBH, Germany
  • Issued date: Between 2003 to 2006
  • Front: Portrait of King Gyanendra, statue of Vishnu at lower left center, Swyambhu Stupa and Harati Temple at center
  • Back: An Asian male elephant
  • Watermark: Plumed crown
  • Size: 70 x 172 mm
