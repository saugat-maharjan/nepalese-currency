  • Domination: Rupees 50
  • Signature:
      • 12-Tilak Bahadur Rawal
      13-Bijaya Nath Bhattarai
  • Printer:
      • -De La Rue & Company, Ltd, UK

      • -Potrait of King Gyanendra with black and blue head design

  • Back:

  • Size:
