Biography:

Krishna Bahadur Manandhar was the acting governor of Nepal. He was the governor from July 2, 2007 to January 15, 2009. Signature of Krishna Bahadur Manandhar can be seen on the currency of Rs 5, Rs 10, Rs 20, Rs 50, Rs 100 & Rs 1000.
