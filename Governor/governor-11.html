Biography:

Dipendra Purush Dhakal was the 11th governor of Nepal. He was the governor from August 29, 2000 to April 27, 2000 during the ruling period of King Birendra. Signatue of Dipendra Purush Dhakal can be seen on the currency of Rs 2, Rs 5, Rs 10, Rs 20, Rs 50, Rs 100, Rs 500 & Rs 1000.
